Our Mission

Sheltering Silverton collaborates with neighbors and organizations to provide a framework that supports, develops and provides services to, and advocates for our vulnerable neighbors in order for them to transition toward fuller security, stability, and purpose within our community. Our programs include our warming shelter, resource center, transitional shelter, food & basic needs, showers and sanitation, and the support of our volunteer advocates.
